How it works

One Pull.
Everything Updates.

01
The 3 AM Pull
n8n pulls your POS data — product mix, sales, labor, comps, schedules — plus vendor invoices and updated price lists. One automated pull while the kitchen sleeps.
02
Prices Recost Themselves
Vendor sends an updated price list. System reads it. Every recipe that touches that ingredient recosts automatically. Your food cost is always current.
03

Summer and winter run differently. Par levels shift as volume picks up or slows. The system watches the patterns. Prep lists and order guides update automatically.
